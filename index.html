About Us

Inaugurated in early 2024, the Developers Hub DIT© is dedicated to advancing and diversifying web development expertise among computer engineering students, both within and beyond the campus confines.
Boasting a membership of over 130 individuals as of June 2024, this intellectual sanctuary has proven to be an invaluable resource for both educators and learners alike.

We extend our deepest appreciation to our mentors and patrons whose guidance has been instrumental in our journey.
